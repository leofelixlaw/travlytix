Ingest

Recognise your data sources. Are they documents, social media, or clickstream logs? If so, ingest your data before you can effectively analyze and enrich it. To make sense of all the data you have, define a structure and correlate the disparate data sets. This important step involves both understanding and standardizing your data.

  • Detect Schema: Identify travel e-commerce schema and metadata that is explicitly defined in headers, fields, or tags

  • Identify Duplicates: Find and flag duplicates in data so to reduce the size of data lake.

  • Statistical Profiling: Create standard statistical analysis of numerical data, and frequency and term analysis of text data.

Process

Crux of data engineering is data processing. After ingesting data, leverage any patterns and knowledge-based classifications to understand the domains found in your data sets.

  • Match: Match unique users in real time to understand in-depth behaviour.

  • Process: Handle multiple formats of data sources, whether their content is structured, semi-structured, or unstructured.

  • Cleanse: Remove non essential characters and standardize date formats.

  • Repair: Find and fix inconsistencies.
