Travlytix Limitations

Travlytix overall limitations:

Travlytix imposes a limitation of maximum 1 communication per channel per 24 hours. This is to ensure passenger is not sent excessive messages due to multiple segments, misconfigurations or any errors. If the same passenger is configured in 2 segments, the campaign which runs first takes precedence.


# Trial Limitations:
1 Trial account can create 10 segments overall
2 Trial account can create 10 campaigns overall
3 Segment contacts not to exceed 100k contacts.
4 Campaign rules engine not to go more than 3 levels deep
