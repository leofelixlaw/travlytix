Campaign Examples

Here are a few basic examples of campaigns that Airlines can run with the help of Travlytix to make their campaigns a lot more personalized. To find out what other, even much more personlized campaigns are possible, please contact us by requesting a demo.

Abandoned Cart
Segment

Target customers who have selected dates and a destination and got all the way to the payment page, filled in their personal and payment details and then abandoned the booking due to various reasons.

Campaign

Send an email/sms or push notification to the customer saying “We have noticed you haven’t booked your trip to (x) destination. Here is a (x) % discount if you book by today”. OR “Book soon as these dates/this destination is selling out fast.”

 Special Offer
Segment

Customer who has booked flights for a long Christmas break over the last three years.

Campaign

Send an Email/SMS or Push Notification with a special Christmas offers saying: ”We have noticed your favourite time of the year to travel is Christmas so we have selected some special Christmas deals especially for you. Enjoy 10% off if you book today!”

Meals
Segment

Target customers who have booked flights without booking a meal.

Campaign

Send them an email/sms or push notification urging that they might have forgotten to buy meals and better to buy right now with a message like: ”We noticed you are going on your trip very soon. Make sure you secure your favourite meal by booking today and save 10%.”

Baggage
Segment

Target customers who have booked return flights for a trip that is longer than a week without booking baggage.

Campaign

Send email/sms or push notification urging that they might have forgotten to buy baggage and better to buy right now with a message like: ”We noticed you are going on a holiday very soon. Make sure you have space for all your belongings and souvenirs by booking baggage now and avoid higher charges at the airport!.”

Insurance
Segment

Customers who have booked tickets without insurance cover.

Campaign

Send email/sms or push notification encouraging to buy insurance with a message like:”Make sure you and your loved ones are well taken care of on your holidays” or “Buy insurance cover today and enjoy 5% off and enjoy your holiday with a peace of mind”.
